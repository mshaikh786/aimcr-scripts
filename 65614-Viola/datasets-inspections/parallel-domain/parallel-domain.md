### Ownership, Source, and Provenance

- **Owner / Curator:** Columbia University, Stanford University, and Toyota Research Institute.
- **Primary Distribution Platform:** https://gcd.cs.columbia.edu/#datasets
- **Repository:** https://github.com/basilevh/gcd (23 commits, 281 stars)
- **Access Method:** Through [download script](https://gcd.cs.columbia.edu/gcd_kubric4d_train_dl.sh)
- **Provenance:** 
- GCD datasets are **derived research benchmarks** created to study **Generalized Category Discovery**, where models must handle:
    - Known classes  
    - Novel classes  
    - Temporally consistent object identities (in videos / 4D scenes)
  - The datasets are **not captured from scratch** by the authors; instead they are:
    - Curated and re-labeled from **existing image, video, and synthetic datasets**
    - Augmented with **new splits, category partitions, and annotations** suitable for GCD evaluation

- **Maintenance Status:** last commit 18 Nov 2025
