### Ownership, Source, and Provenance

- **Owner / Curator:** Originally founded as Mapillary AB; acquired by Meta in 2020
- **Primary Distribution Platform:** https://www.mapillary.com/dataset/depth
- **Repository:** https://github.com/mapillary
- **Access Method:** Via download link on [website](https://www.mapillary.com/dataset/depth)
- **Provenance:** 
    - **Crowdsourced, street-level imagery** contributed by a global community of users (dashcams, action cameras, smartphones).  
    - Images are **geotagged and timestamped**, then processed by Mapillary to extract map-relevant features (roads, signs, lanes, sidewalks).  
    - Data reflects **real-world public spaces**, captured under diverse geographic, lighting, and weather conditions.  
    - Human faces and license plates are **automatically blurred** for privacy prior to distribution.
