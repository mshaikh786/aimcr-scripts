## Sample Representation
- **Unit of Data:** One DFT-evaluated material structure
- **Core Fields:**
  - Atomic species
  - Atomic positions
  - Cell / lattice information
- **Labels / Targets:**
  - Total energy (eV)
  - Atomic forces (eV/Å)
  - Stress tensor (eV/Å³)
- **Metadata Included:**
  - Calculation type (single-point or relaxation)
  - Subdataset identifier (input generation strategy)
